LA ROSALÍA

Rosalía is a Spanish singer-songwriter from Barcelona. She is a world renowned artist who has transcended genres of music by creating new sounds that fuse flamenco, trap, reggaeton, and pop beats. Her roots in Spanish culture have combined with her interests in contemporary pop culture to develop music unlike any other. She has collaborated with well known artists across all genres such as Pharrell Williams, Ozuna, Travis Scott, and James Blake. Rosalía has been awarded 2 Latin Grammys for Best Urban Fusion/performance and Best Alternative Song.
